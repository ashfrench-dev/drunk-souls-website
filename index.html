Drunk Souls: A Dark Souls Drinking Game

Ready to stumble through the world of Lordran with a drink in hand and a fire in your belly? Drunk Souls transforms your Dark Souls gaming sessions into legendary party nights with friends. Grab your drinks, summon your bravest companions, and prepare to test your skills against Lordran's most notorious challenges. Remember, in this game, the real boss is your sobriety! May your aim be as sharp as a Dragonslayer's spear and your tolerance as sturdy as Black Iron Tarkus. Cheers to epic quests and even more epic fails!

Definitions

  • Drink: A simple sip of whatever drink the player has.
  • Big Drink: A large swig of the player's drink or something stronger like a shot of liquor.

Setup

  • Setup: Gather your friends and set up Dark Souls. Feel free to adapt these rules for other From Software games like Bloodborne, Elden Ring, or any of the sequel titles.
  • Prepare: Prepare drinks and snacks.
  • Understand the Rules: Make sure everyone understands the rules.
  • Enjoy: Enjoy the game and drink responsibly!

Game Start

  • Character Creation: Feel free to pick any starting class or gifts, but starting with the Deprived class is recommended for best effect.
  • Game Start: When the game starts or after the initial character creation, all players do a cheers with 1 big drink and say, "Prepare to Die!"
  • Starting Player: The starting player is the person with the least amount of experience with the Dark Souls series.

Basic Rules

Death Rules

Death Rules — Action, Drink, and other notes
Action Drink Other notes
Die to a Boss 1 drink Pass the controller
Die to a Regular Enemy 1 drink Pass the controller
Die from Environment (Falling, Traps, Poison/Toxic) 2 drinks Pass the controller

Bonfire Rules

Bonfire Rules — Action, Drink, and other notes
Action Drink Other notes
Light a New Bonfire 1 drink Everyone drinks; Rest at bonfire or pass the controller
Rest at a Bonfire 1 drink Pass the controller after finishing Bonfire menu actions
Kindle a Bonfire 1 drink

Item/Action Rules

Item/Action Rules — Action, Drink, and other notes
Action Drink Other notes
Use an Estus Flask 1 drink
Summon Help (NPC or Player) 1 drink
Use a Humanity/Hollowing Item 1 drink

Boss Rules

Boss Rules — Action, Drink, and other notes
Action Drink Other notes
Defeat a Boss 1 big drink Everyone else drinks

Special Rules

Special Rules — Action, Drink, and other notes
Action Drink Other notes
Find a Hidden Wall or Secret 1 drink Choose another player to drink
Get Cursed 1 big drink
Lose Souls (by dying again before retrieving) 1 drink Both you and the previous player drink
Discover a New Area 1 drink Everyone drinks

Challenge Rules

Challenge Rules — Action, Drink, and other notes
Action Drink Other notes
No Hit Challenge (Complete a Level or Boss Without Taking Damage) 1 big drink Success: everyone else drinks; Failure: you drink
